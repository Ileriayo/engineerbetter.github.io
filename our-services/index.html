Our services 

We offer holistic services to help you deliver value faster. See the ways we can help you from training, to consultancy and enablement through delivery.

Instructor-led training

We deliver these hands-on, on-site, instructor-led courses:

  •  4 day CF Certified Developer
  •  1 day CF Zero To Hero
  •  1 day Concourse Basics

EngineerBetter authored the official CF Certified Developer training programme, delivered the Zero To Hero course at CF Summit Europe, as well as to many direct customers.

Days

Immersive training

A novel training course bridging the gap between one-to-one mentoring of pair-programming, and more scalable one-to-many knowledge transfer of instructor-led training.

  •  1 week
  •  Mob Programming in small groups

In only five days a team with no prior experience of cloud-native platforms or development can deliver a working solution for their business.

Weeks

Enablement through delivery

We enable customers to deliver business value faster with Cloud Foundry or Kubernetes, by pair-programming with their engineers over a number of months. We work from an ordered backlog, continuously delivering usable solutions into production.

  •  Multi-month engagement
  •  Pair programming in your team

Pair-programming is the most successful method for increasing knowledge in our customers' businesses whilst also delivering production systems.

Months

Consultancy or Coaching

We advise and give guidance on the use of our core technology, continuous delivery of cloud-native microservices, or effective team working process.

At larger scale we provide coaching that helps teams and leaders reveal a path towards the organisational change required to deliver business value faster.

  •  1-4 days per week
  •  Dedicated time with our most experienced consultants

While consulting we’ve used our expertise to prepare organisations’ agile transformations, platform migrations, and produced product roadmaps.
